Our services

Travel Photography

We offer a travel-based Photography for brands, tourism projects, and promotional materials. This includes environmental shots, cultural documentaion and location-based imagery. The projects are approached withe flexibiliry depending on the setting, with the importance of working within the environment naturally instead of staging scenes.

Automotive Photography

Automotive photography involves capturing the essence and details of vehicles, highlighting their design, features, and unique characteristics in a professional and visually appealing manner. These services can be offered to individuals, brands and dealerships. This further implies that the work involves static shots, rolling shots, and detail-focused images depending on the specific requirements of the project. The sessions are planned aroundf the location, lighting, and the type of visuals required. Furthermore, each shoot is approached with control over the angles, reflections, and the environment to ensure the quality of the service provided to the clients.

Wedding Photography

We offer full and partial wedding coverage. This implies that the coverage is structured around your timeline, with flexibility to adapt as the day unfolds. This includes couple portraits, venue decor, family shots, and natural documentation of the event. The focus is on capturing the event as it happens, without excessive direction or interference.

Food Photography

We provide food photography services for restaurants, cafes, and food bloggers. This includes menu images, styled shots, and product-based imagery. The shoots can be conducted on-site or in a controlled setup, which depends on the look the client wants. Furthermore, the attention is given to the lighting, consistency, and presentation to ensure the highest quality of the final images.
